# Privacy Policy / RowSync
Your privacy is critically important to us. Your data is your own. We make no claims to it and will only use it according to the policies outlined below.

## Information we collect
RowSync does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

RowSync accesses your Augletics sign-in details only while signing you in. RowSync does not store your Augletics password. The app stores authentication tokens in the iOS keychain so it can refresh your session.

RowSync requests workout IDs and workout details from your Augletics account so you can choose which workouts to sync to Apple Health.

### HealthKit
If you allow RowSync to integrate with HealthKit, the application will access only the health data types you opt in to use.

RowSync may write indoor rowing workouts, distance, energy, heart rate, and related workout metadata to Apple Health when you choose to sync.

RowSync may read limited Apple Health workout metadata created by RowSync to identify workouts that were already synced and avoid duplicate entries.

In no case is your health data transferred to a third party without your explicit consent.

### Support files
If RowSync cannot understand a workout format, it may offer to prepare a support package. That package can include raw workout JSON and may contain sensitive training data. RowSync shares those files only when you explicitly choose to email or share them.

By using our apps, you consent to our privacy policy.
